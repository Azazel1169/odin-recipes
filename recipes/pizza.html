Classic Homemade Pizza

This homemade pizza recipe gives you a deliciously crispy crust, gooey mozzarella cheese, and a rich tomato sauce that’ll transport you back to Friday night pizza parties in the early 2000s.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix and knead until dough is smooth.
  3. Cover and let rise for about 1 hour in a warm place.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and place it on a pizza pan.
  6. Spread sauce, add cheese, and top with your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let cool slightly, slice it up, and enjoy your pizza party throwback!
