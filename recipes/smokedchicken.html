Smoked Chicken Recipe

Make the perfect Smoked Chicken Thighs every time by following my easy recipe! Using only 5 ingredients including salt and pepper, this easy recipe for smoked chicken is ready in under 3 hours with 10 minutes of prep.

Ingredients

  • Bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs, boneless skinless chicken thighs, or chicken breasts
  • BBQ Sauce – the choice of your favorite barbecue sauce – I like to use my Homemade BBQ Sauce
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Kosher Salt and cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the smoker to 225°F. Salt and pepper on both sides of the chicken thighs. Do not be shy with the seasoning!
  2. Place your chicken on the smoker, skin side down and let them smoke for 1 hour.
  3. While the chicken smokes, mix the BBQ sauce with apple cider vinegar. You are just looking for a thinner sauce consistency for easy glazing.
  4. After the first hour of smoking, bump the temp up to 300°F and let the chicken smoke for another hour, or until the internal temperature of the chicken hits 185°F on a meat thermometer. Once the chicken has finished cooking, dunk them in the glaze and cover them entirely.
  5. Then place them back on the smoker for another half hour, or until the glaze is nice and tacky.
  6. Remove the chicken from the smoker. Let the chicken rest for about ten minutes and enjoy!
