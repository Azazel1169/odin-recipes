Homemade Lasagna

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat: ½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef
  • Onion: One diced onion
  • Canned tomatoes: Tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs: 2 tbsp parsley and 1 clove garlic
  • Sugar: A dash to balance acidity
  • Spices: Dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper
  • Noodles: Uncooked or oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese, Parmesan, and shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs: For creaminess and binding

Steps to Make Homemade Lasagna

  1. Cook the meat: Brown the meat in a skillet. Add onion and cook until translucent. Stir in tomatoes, half the parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil noodles in salted water until al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan, eggs, the rest of the parsley, remaining salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer sauce, noodles, cheese, repeat. End with mozzarella.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake until top is golden.
